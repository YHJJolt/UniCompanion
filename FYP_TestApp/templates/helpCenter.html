🧾 Account & Profile

Click the "Sign Up" button at the top-right corner and follow the instructions to register using your email.
Go to your account settings and update your profile image or display name under the "Edit Profile" section.
Navigate to your account settings and click "Delete Account" at the bottom. This action is permanent.

💬 Posting & Comments

Use the comment box under any post or discussion. Press enter or click "Post" to submit your comment.
Reputation points are earned when other users upvote your comments. Higher reputation unlocks community privileges.
Comments may be removed for violating community guidelines (e.g. spam, abuse, or off-topic content). Check our rules page for details.

⚠️ Moderation & Safety

Click the three-dot menu next to any post or comment and select "Report". Provide a reason, and our team will review it.
Depending on the violation, you may receive a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban.
Moderators are selected based on reputation, activity, and behavior in the community. Admins may also appoint trusted users.
